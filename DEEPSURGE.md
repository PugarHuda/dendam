### Project Name → description box (rich text — formatting is supported)
> **Dendam** is a grudge-holding **World Cup 2026** rival AI with genuine persistent memory on **Walrus Memory** (Sui Mainnet). Throw it your predictions, hot takes and trash talk — it stores every one and throws them back the moment you are wrong.
>
> Every turn runs a **Recall → Respond → Remember** loop:
> - **Recall** what it already knows about you from Walrus (semantic search).
> - **Respond** in character as a vengeful rival, quoting your past self word-for-word, in your own language.
> - **Remember** the new takes back to Walrus for next time.
>
> The before/after is provable: on **day one it admits it has nothing on you** — a hard anti-fabrication guard means it cannot invent a fake past — and after a few sessions it weaponizes your file. When real match results land, it auto-stamps your wrong predictions **WRONG**, permanently, on-chain.
>
> The memory is **visible and verifiable**: *The File* and public *share cards* show every memory as a real **Walrus blob** linked to a MemWalAccount on Sui. It is also multiplayer — a **Hall of Shame** leaderboard ranked purely from stored memory, and **Match Rooms** where Dendam joins a cross-user chat using what each person actually said.
>
> **Play instantly as a guest (just a nickname), or connect a Sui wallet to truly own your File.**
